Mission Objective
Question 1: Which area is most suitable for long-term life?
Question 2: Which villa/home is the strongest candidate?
Output: A ranked, scored decision based on field observations, not memory or emotion.
Daily Recon Missions
Hua Hin Orientation: Confirm food, 7-Eleven, beach access, ATM, route safety, and basic walkability.
Area Scoring: Score Nong Kae, Soi 94, Bluport area, Market Village area, west villa areas, and any driver/agent recommendation.
Property Scoring: Score each villa/home individually. Do not rely on community reputation alone.
Medical Day: Evaluate hospital, pharmacy, lab, emergency access, and specialty-care practicality.
Budget Day: Price meals, groceries, laundry, transport, rent, utilities, and gym access.
Pattaya Comparison: Use the same scoring model against Pattaya/Jomtien to compare fairly.
Area Evaluations
Score each area separately. Use 0–10. Totals and rankings update automatically.
Property Evaluations
Score each home/villa/condo/community separately. Capture address, rent, stats, notes, and final decision.
