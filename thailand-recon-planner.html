BKK → Kiang Haad Beach Hua Hin by Train
Use this if choosing rail instead of private car. This requires moving from Suvarnabhumi Airport into Bangkok’s rail system before continuing south to Hua Hin.
Land at BKK at 5:05 AM.
Follow signs for Immigration / Passport Control.
Clear immigration, collect baggage, and exit customs.
Go to Basement Level B for the Airport Rail Link.
Purchase Airport Rail Link token/ticket.
Board the Airport Rail Link City Line.
Ride from Suvarnabhumi Airport to Makkasan Station.
Walk/transfer to MRT Phetchaburi Station.
Board MRT Blue Line toward Bang Sue / Krung Thep Aphiwat.
Exit at Krung Thep Aphiwat Central Terminal.
Purchase or collect train ticket for Hua Hin.
Board southbound train to Hua Hin Railway Station.
Ride train to Hua Hin.
Exit Hua Hin Railway Station.
Open Grab or use taxi queue outside station.
Travel to Kiang Haad Beach Hua Hin, 131/105 Petchkasem Rd, Hua Hin.
Expected total travel time: approximately 5–7 hours depending transfers and train schedule.
