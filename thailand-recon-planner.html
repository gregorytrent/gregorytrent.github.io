Primary Plan: BKK → Hua Hin Direct
Current hotel structure sends you directly from Bangkok Suvarnabhumi Airport to Hua Hin on June 5. This is the preferred plan because your first confirmed hotel night is already in Hua Hin.
Land at BKK at 5:05 AM.
Deplane and follow signs for Immigration / Passport Control.
Estimated timing: 5:15–5:35 AM for deplaning and walking to immigration.
Clear immigration. Estimated timing: 5:35–6:15 AM.
Go to baggage claim and customs. Estimated timing: 6:15–6:45 AM.
Exit customs into the public arrivals area.
Get cash, SIM/data, and water before leaving the airport.
Meet premium private driver or go to the official transport desk.
Transfer directly to Kiang Haad Beach Hua Hin.
Target arrival in Hua Hin: roughly 9:30 AM–11:30 AM depending transport and traffic.
Backup Plan: Airport Rail Link to Bangkok City
Use this only if a Bangkok stop becomes necessary. The front-end Bangkok overnight has been removed from the current plan.
After customs, enter the public arrivals area.
Follow signs for Airport Rail Link / Train to City.
Take elevator or escalator down to Basement Level B.
Buy token/ticket at the machine or counter.
Reach Airport Rail Link around 6:45–7:00 AM.
Likely boarding window: 7:00–7:15 AM.
Board the Airport Rail Link City Line.
Use Makkasan Station for: Sukhumvit / Asok / Nana / Phrom Phong.
Use Phaya Thai Station for: Siam / Phaya Thai / Victory Monument.
Best luggage strategy: BKK → Makkasan by train → Grab/taxi to hotel.
